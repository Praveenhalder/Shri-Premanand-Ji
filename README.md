# Shri Premanand Ji
Shri Premanand Ji - Open Source Android App

A beautiful mobile-spiritual app dedicated to **Shri Premanand Ji Maharaj** — carrying his pravachans, the Bhagavad Gita, and the fragrance of Vrindavan wherever you go.

---

## ✨ What's Inside


### 🎙️ Pravachan — प्रवचन सुनें
- Listen to pravachans of Shri Premanand Ji Maharaj
- Full audio player with waveform, seek bar, and timestamps
- **Search** — find any pravachan instantly by name
- **Favorites ★** — mark your beloved pravachans; they always appear at the top
- **Download ⬇** — save pravachans to your device and listen fully offline, even without internet
- New pravachans are fetched automatically and shown with a **नया** badge
- Resumes exactly where you left off — even after closing and reopening the app
- Favorite and download buttons are right on the player, no need to scroll

---

### 📖 Bhagavad Gita — भगवद् गीता

**In-app Reader**
- All 18 chapters with original Sanskrit verses
- Hindi translation (भावार्थ) for every shlok
- English translation alongside
- Chapter progress bar — jump to any chapter instantly
- Remembers the last chapter you were reading

**Original PDF**
- Read the complete Bhagavad Gita as the original PDF
- **Pinch to zoom** — zoom in to read comfortably, zoom out to see the full page
- **Double-tap** to zoom in on any spot; double-tap again to reset
- **Pan** freely when zoomed in
- Download once, read forever — works completely offline
- Remembers the exact page you were on
